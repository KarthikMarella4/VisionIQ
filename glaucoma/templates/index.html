About VisionIQ

VisionIQ tackles the "silent thief of sight" - Glaucoma. By leveraging deep learning architectures, we process retinal fundus images to detect pathological changes with high accuracy. Our system analyzes key biomarkers like the cup-to-disc ratio to distinguish between healthy and glaucomatous eyes, democratizing access to expert-level screening.
